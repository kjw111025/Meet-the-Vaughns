Our Love Story

Tyler and Darius met in September 2015 at Eastern Michigan University in an Audio Engineering class. What started as a simple connection turned into something unforgettable.

Life took them on separate paths for a time, but their story wasn’t finished. In 2021, they found their way back to each other stronger, wiser, and more in love.

On May 30, 2021, their new chapter began. On January 18, 2026, Darius asked Tyler to be his wife. And now, forever begins.
